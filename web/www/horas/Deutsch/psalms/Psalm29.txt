# Psalm 29:1 (psalmus cantici in dedicatione domus David)
# A song of praise for the dedication of David's house

# Psalm 29:2 exaltabo te Domine quoniam suscepisti me nec delectasti inimicos meos super me
# I will extol you, Lord, for you have raised me up and have not let my enemies rejoice over me

# Psalm 29:3 Domine Deus meus clamavi ad te et sanasti me
# Lord my God, I cried out to you and you healed me

# Psalm 29:4 Domine eduxisti ab inferno animam meam salvasti me a descendentibus in lacum
# Lord, you have brought my soul up from Sheol, you have saved me from those who go down to the pit

# Psalm 29:5 psallite Domino sancti eius et confitemini memoriae sanctitatis eius
# Sing to the Lord, you his holy ones, and give praise to his memory

# Psalm 29:6 quoniam ira in indignatione eius et vita in voluntate eius ad vesperum demorabitur fletus et ad matutinum laetitia
# For his anger lasts only a moment, but his favor lasts a lifetime; weeping may stay for the night, but rejoicing comes in the morning

# Psalm 29:7 ego autem dixi in abundantia mea non movebor in aeternum
# I thought, "I will never be moved; I will never be shaken."

# Psalm 29:8 Domine in voluntate tua praestitisti decori meo virtutem avertisti faciem tuam et factus sum conturbatus
# Lord, by your favor you made my mountain stand strong; you hid your face and I was dismayed
