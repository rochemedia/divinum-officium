124:1 De, der stoler på Herren, er som Zions bjerg, * det rokkes ikke, det står fast for evigt.  // This line suggests that those who trust in the Lord are like the steadfast and enduring Mount Zion.
124:2 Bjerge omgiver Jerusalem, * og Herren omgiver sit folk fra nu af og til evig tid.  // This line emphasizes the protection and security provided by the Lord to his people, just as mountains surround Jerusalem.
124:3 Nej, uretfærdighedens scepter skal ikke hvile over de retfærdiges arvelod, * så de retfærdige rækker deres hånd til uret.  // This line asserts that injustice shall not prevail over the inheritance of the righteous, who will instead reach out against wrongdoing.
124:4 Herre, vær god mod de gode * og mod de oprigtige!  // This line is a plea for the Lord to show kindness and goodness to the righteous and the sincere.
124:5 Men dem, der går krogveje, skal Herren føre bort sammen med forbryderne. * Fred over Israel!  // This line calls for the Lord to remove those who take crooked paths along with the transgressors, and it concludes with a blessing of peace over Israel.
